Example management packages

completely flexible according to your needs

Below are some examples of different WeMakeGyms management packages

Home gym maintenance

Induction session: A fully qualified fitness professional will provide expert advice on efficient and safe use of all installed equipment.

Personal Training: Our fully qualified, experienced personal trainers can offer a regular training service at a competitive rate.

Gym maintenance: Tailored maintenance packages are available to make sure your gym continues to perform at its best.

Goals and equipment review: We can schedule regular reviews of your fitness goals and make recommendations as to the latest equipment updates and improvements.

Corporate & Commercial gym management

A comprehensive range of management services to add value to your site and generate ongoing revenue.

Staffing: We are able to recruit, train and manage a range of fully qualified gym staff including personal trainers, instructors and gym managers (employed or self-employed). We will arrange inductions by qualified fitness professionals (minimum of level 3 personal trainer) to meet health and safety regulations.

Operations: Our comprehensive operations packages ensure the safe and efficient day-to-day running of fitness facilities including technical support, service visits, cleaning and safety checks. We can optimise the service you provide for members – designing and running a bespoke schedule of cutting-edge classes as well as coordinating PT schedules, managing customer feedback and monitoring facility usage.

Financial management: We offer an umbrella service managing all gym-related administration and financial exchanges for corporate and commercial clients. This can include membership fees, staff payroll, rental fees and equipment leasing.
